Why ChainFunding?
Combining the concepts of Blockchain and Crowdfunding, the Chain X Funding platform was born. Based on individual willingness to invest in proportion to the ratio of funds, it aims to solve the problem of high learning costs for NFT investment beginners, blockchain transaction fees and various NFT products. Generally, the amount is relatively high.

For people who want to launch a crowdfunding project
Do you feel that your grand investment dreams are limited by your financial power? The first step to realizing your dreams on 'ChainFunding' is to determine your project goals. What are you looking for in an investment target? What are some good crowdfunding strategies? If you can interest other investors in your crowdfunding project and reach the threshold and goals within the crowdfunding period, you can gather the public's funds and power here to achieve your investment dreams together!

For people who want to participate in a crowdfunding project
Each project will be set by the initiator with an investment strategy, including stop profit, stop loss price, crowdfunding period, etc. You can view a variety of crowdfunding projects on this platform, easily realizing the concept of collective investment, collective enjoyment of profit, and collective acceptance of loss risk through joining the projects you like. If the project fails because the fundraising progress is not as expected, the amount invested will be returned to your account without any fees!
